Kathryn McClintock

Kathryn McClintock is a design-oriented front-end developer. She strives to build immersive and beautiful web applications through carefully crafted code and user-centric design.

Since graduating with a B.A. in Communication from Trinity University in 2007, Kathryn has focused her professional career almost exclusively on Drupal development.

She is the Team Lead for Amazee Labs Austin and specializes in Drupal's front-end. Kathryn is especially interested in Sass, Twig, and Styled Components.

In addition to her development work, Kathryn is a long-time member of the North American DrupalCon Program Team and has served on the front-end track for Austin ('14), Los Angeles ('15), New Orleans ('16), and Baltimore ('17).

When not online, Kathryn can be found in Austin, Texas with her husband, Andrew — searching for the perfect vintage items for their home, trying out a new recipe in the kitchen, experimenting in gardening, or snuggling with their two dogs, Jackson and Pan.
